Standard Rules:<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

    \n
  • Only league bats may be used. If a bat is approved, must be available for both teams.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Games are 9 innings with a starting count of 1 ball and 1 strike.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Batters are allowed one foul regardless of count, second foul is automatic out.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • A second foul out is a dead ball, runners cannot advance whether ball is caught or not.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • If the first game starts after 9:05 AM no new inning will start after 10:15 AM.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • All players must play 4 innings minimum in the field. All players must be in batting order.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Base runners must try to avoid contact on basepaths, any deliberate attempt to make contact will result<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • in a called out and possible double play call, the decision of umpire is final.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Any contact between runner and fielder at home plate without a slide will be an automatic out.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • If there is an attempt to make a play at first base, runner must touch orange part of bag first.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • No children are allowed in the playing area or dugouts for their own safety. This rule will be strictly enforced.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n\n

    Picking up Subs:<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

      \n
    • If a team picks up players, they must give up two runs per player (6 runs max).<\/li>\n\n\n\n
    • Any team picking up three or more players must play with nine players.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
    • If a team picks up any players in playoff games, they must play with a max of nine players.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
    • Teams must bat their own players first.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
    • Any player who gets picked up must be replaced as soon as the team\u2019s players arrive.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
    • Teams may elect to keep picked up player in as a DH until team has 11 of their own players.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n\n

      Mercy Rule:<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

        \n
      • The mercy rule is 12 or more runs after 8 innings.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
      • Flip\/Flop Rule becomes invoked if home team is losing by 12 or more runs at the end of the 7% inning.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n\n

        Flip-Flop Run Rule:<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

          \n
        • At the end of the 7th inning, if the home team is losing by 12 or more runs this constitutes a team flip.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
        • The home team will become the visiting team and vice versa. The visiting team will remain on the field.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
        • The home team will now bat in the top of the 8t inning. If enough runs are scored to get out of mercy,<\/li>\n\n\n\n
        • The game will continue, otherwise, the game is over.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
        • If the game continues, the visiting team will now bat bottom of the 8″ inning. If the visiting team scores enough runs to cause mercy, the game is over, No 9th inning is played.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n\n

          Sportsmanship:<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

            \n
          • Unsportsmanlike conduct will not be tolerated.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
          • First violation = player will receive a warning from the umpire.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
          • Second violation = player will be ejected from game and suspended for one game.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
          • Any flagrant violations will result in immediate ejection and additional one game suspension.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n\n

            League Dues:<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

              \n
            • League dues must be paid before the season begins, and is a requirement to participate in the league. Dues can be paid as early as your time of signup for the league, meaning months before the first game is played.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
            • League dues must be paid by either cash or electronic (Venmo, Cash App, Zelle) to your coach.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
