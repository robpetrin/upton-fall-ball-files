Rules of Play<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

Standard Rules:<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

    \n
  • Only league bats may be used. If a bat is approved, must be available for both teams.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Games are 9 innings with a starting count of 1 ball and 1 strike.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Batters are allowed one foul regardless of count, second foul is automatic out.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • A second foul out is a dead ball, runners cannot advance whether ball is caught or not.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • If the first game starts after 9:05 AM no new inning will start after 10:15 AM (exception for playoff games).<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • All players must play 4 innings minimum in the field. All players must be in batting order.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Base runners must try to avoid contact on basepaths, any deliberate attempt to make contact will result in a called out and possible double play call, the decision of umpire is final.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Any contact between runner and fielder at home plate without a slide will be an automatic out.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • If there is an attempt to make a play at first base, runner must touch orange part of bag first, and the fielder must use the white part of the bag.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • No children are allowed in the playing area or dugouts for their own safety. This rule will be strictly enforced.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n\n

    Picking up Subs:<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

      \n
    • If a team picks up players, they must give up two runs per player (6 runs max).<\/li>\n\n\n\n
    • Any team picking up three or more players must play with nine players.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
    • If a team picks up any players in playoff games, they must play with a max of nine players.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
    • Teams must bat their own players first.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
    • Any player who gets picked up must be replaced as soon as the team\u2019s players arrive.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
    • Teams may elect to keep picked up player in as a DH until team has 11 of their own players.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n\n

      Mercy Rule:<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

        \n
      • The mercy rule is 12 or more runs after 8 innings.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
      • Flip\/Flop Rule becomes invoked if home team is losing by 12 or more runs at the end of the 7th inning.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n\n

        Flip-Flop Run Rule:<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

          \n
        • At the end of the 7th inning, if the home team is losing by 12 or more runs this constitutes a team flip.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
        • The home team will become the visiting team and vice versa. The visiting team will remain on the field.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
        • The home team will now bat in the top of the 8th inning. If enough runs are scored to get out of mercy, the game will continue, otherwise, the game is over.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
        • If the game continues, the visiting team will now bat bottom of the 8″ inning. If the visiting team scores enough runs to cause mercy, the game is over, No 9th inning is played.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n\n

          League Dues:<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

            \n
          • League dues must be paid before the season begins, and is a requirement to participate in the league. Dues can be paid as early as upon drafting.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
          • League dues must be paid by either cash or electronic (Venmo, Cash App, Zelle) to Gregg Katz. Your spot will be reserved in a first come first serve basis once league dues are paid. Please send dues to Gregg via Venmo at @gkatz17.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
          • You will not be issued a t-shirt if you have not paid your dues, and you will be ineligible for Week 1 of the season.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
          • If you have not paid your dues by the start of play on Week 2, you will be removed from the league and replaced on your team roster by a waitlisted player.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n\n

            Code of Conduct<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

            As a player in the Upton Men’s Fall Ball league, I agree that I…<\/p>\n\n\n\n

              \n
            1. Will Fulfill My Financial Obligations Promptly<\/strong>\n
                \n
              • I will pay any dues or fees on time (ideally at signup, required before the draft, to Gregg Katz).<\/li>\n\n\n\n
              • I acknowledge I will not receive a team shirt and will be barred from participation if I do not meet this expectation.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
              • Any player(s) who have not paid by the morning of Week 2 will be removed from the league and replaced by a waitlisted player.<\/mark><\/li>\n<\/ul>\n<\/li>\n\n\n\n
              • Will Prioritize Fun and Relaxation<\/strong>\n
                  \n
                • I understand that the primary goal of this league is to have fun and relax, not to win at all costs.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
                • I understand that no participant is in control of the weather. While the league will attempt to play all games before it get too cold, decisions to play or not play on any given week are made in the name of safety.<\/mark><\/li>\n<\/ul>\n<\/li>\n\n\n\n
                • Will Show Respect to All Participants<\/strong>\n
                    \n
                  • I will not engage in fights.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
                  • I will not use profanity\/slurs towards coaches, umpires, or any other participants.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
                  • I will not verbally ridicule or belittle other players.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
                  • I will recognize that coaches often double as game officials for other teams.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
                  • I will not consistently criticize game officials, who are volunteering their time.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n<\/li>\n\n\n\n
                  • Understand the Consequences of Misconduct<\/strong>\n
                      \n
                    • I acknowledge that there is more demand for the league than playing spots available, and if I run afoul of this code of conduct, I will be replaced.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
                    • Any player removed\/replaced for any violation of Rule 3 will not be allowed to participate in future seasons of Fall Ball.<\/mark><\/li>\n<\/ul>\n<\/li>\n<\/ol>\n\n\n\n
